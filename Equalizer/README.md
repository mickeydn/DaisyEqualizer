# Equalizer

## Description

Implements a four-band stereo equalizer with peak EQ and high/low shelf bands.

Switch 1 turns the equalizer on/off (LED 1).

Switch 2 selects the EQ band. LED 2 shows the active EQ band (Red band 1, Green band 2, Blue band 3, White band 4, Purple band 5, Gold band 6 )

The encoder adjusts the EQ gain of the active EQ band in the interval 0.1 - 6.0.

Band 1 - Low shelf

band 2-5 peak EQ

band 6 High shelf

The EQ settings and processing timer are transmitted over a USB COM port at 9600 BAUD.
